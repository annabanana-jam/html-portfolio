The Best Movies According to Anna

My top 3 movies of all-time. (According to My Husband)

If I were asked, "Hey Anna, in your opinion, what are the three best movies of all-time?" I would probably have to take a long minute to think about that. I have movies that I love, I have movies that I watch when I don't know what to watch, I have movies that give me the comfort of nostalgia, and then I have movies that I think make me sound super cool and smart. So, since this is a page on my website, I will share the movies that my husband listed as my top three most watched movies of all-time. I don't know whether I should cringe, laugh, or feel honored that he knows me so well...

  1. Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone or Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

    2. There was a time when I was in college and my early career was just starting out. I would come home and put on Harry Potter one or two, and just marinate in the comfort of these movies. Something about the first Harry Potter movies just makes you feel cozy and safe. Especially when you had a rough day at school or work.

  2. Bridget Jones's Diary

    3. We had this movie on DvD, and I used to watch it with my mom all the time as a kid. I of course didn't understand the references or what was really going on, but I loved the humor, and enjoyed watching the dreamy British actors, Colin Firth and Hugh Grant

  3. The Holiday

    4. As I am writing out this last point, I realize there is a very clear theme. They are all British, and mostly romantic comedies. Well, The Holiday is one of those movies that I watch every year around Christmas. It's another movie that I watched with my mom as a kid, and loved it immediately. Like the other two movies, I will never get tired of this one. The way the characters are connected, and the humor makes this a great feel-good movie.
